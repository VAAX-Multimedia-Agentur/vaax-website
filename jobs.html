Position: Freelance 3D Artist (Part-time)

Specialization: Environment Design and Prop Creation

Job Description:
We are actively seeking junior 3D artists, students, and self-taught individuals to join our team as freelance 3D artists, focusing on environment design and prop creation. This is an excellent entry-level opportunity for those looking to break into the industry. We welcome candidates who can deliver 3D models in standardized FBX format with PBR textures, regardless of the software used. Whether you prefer Blender, Cinema4D, Maya, Modo, or any other software, what matters most is the quality and performance of the assets.
Key Responsibilities:
  • Create high-quality 3D models for environments and props.
  • Deliver assets in FBX format with PBR textures.
  • Demonstrate a strong understanding of UV mapping.
  • Utilize trim sheets for texturing to ensure high performance and reusability of textures.
  • Develop modular 3D designs and tilesets for efficient asset creation.
Requirements:
  • Ability to produce 3D models in FBX format with PBR textures.
  • Proficiency in UV mapping and texturing based on trim sheets.
  • Knowledge of modular 3D design and tilesets is highly desirable.
  • Knowledge of the Unity engine is an advantage but not necessary.
  • Strong communication skills in English, written preferred; no verbal skills required.
  • No need for a resume or prior industry experience; however, a portfolio showcasing your work is required.
  • Self-taught artists are highly encouraged to apply.
What We Offer:
  • Freelance position with flexible, part-time hours.
  • Opportunity to work on a project basis.
  • We welcome applications from individuals who are deaf, mute, or autistic.
  • Ideal for junior applicants, students, beginners, and self-taught artists looking to build their portfolio.
  • A work environment that minimizes verbal communication, making it suitable for individuals with autism and others who prefer written communication, allowing us to focus fully on our creative process.
Application Process:
If you are a passionate junior artist, student, or self-taught individual eager to gain experience in the 3D art field, we encourage you to apply. Please submit your portfolio for review.
